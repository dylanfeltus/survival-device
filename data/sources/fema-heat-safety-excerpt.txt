HEAT EXHAUSTION
Signs include heavy sweating, weakness, cold/clammy skin, fast weak pulse, nausea or vomiting, and fainting. Move the person to a cooler place, loosen clothing, apply cool wet cloths, and give sips of water if conscious.

HEAT STROKE
Heat stroke is a medical emergency. Signs include high body temperature, hot red skin, rapid strong pulse, and possible unconsciousness. Call emergency services and cool the person rapidly.
