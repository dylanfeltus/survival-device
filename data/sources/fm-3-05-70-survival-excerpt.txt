WATER PROCUREMENT
Boiling is the best method of purifying water. At sea level, bring water to a rolling boil for 1 minute. At higher elevations, boil longer. Filtering removes sediments but does not remove all pathogens; disinfect after filtering when possible.

SOLAR STILL
A solar still can produce water from moist soil or vegetation. Dig a hole, place a container in the center, cover with plastic, and place a small rock in the center so condensation drips into the container.
